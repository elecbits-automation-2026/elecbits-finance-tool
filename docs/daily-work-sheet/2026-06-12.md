## 3. Enforce the budget approval workflow server-side (RLS + trigger)
**Commit:** `8d766bb`

Closes the "every rule is client-side only" caveat from
`docs/AUDIT-budget-workflow-test.md` Â§6 item 8 for the `budgets` table.

- **Migration 0011** (also merged into `schema.sql`) replaces the permissive

  - a `budgets_guard` AFTER INSERT/UPDATE/DELETE trigger mirroring
    `getEligibleDeptApprovers` / `computeNextStage` / `canUserActOnRequest` and
